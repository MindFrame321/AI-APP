Frequently Asked Questions

How does Focufy work?
Focufy uses AI to analyze web pages and block distracting elements while keeping content relevant to your study goal visible. Just enter your goal and start a focus session!
Is there a free trial?
Yes! You get a 7-day free trial with full premium features. No credit card required.
How does YouTube blocking work?
Focufy analyzes video titles and descriptions to show only videos related to your study goal. Unrelated videos are automatically hidden.
Can I use Focufy on multiple devices?
Currently, Focufy works on Chrome. Cloud sync across devices is coming soon for Premium users.
What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Free: 3 sessions/day, basic blocking. Premium: Unlimited sessions, advanced AI, full analytics, cloud sync, and more.
How do I contact support?
Use the AI assistant above, or email prithivponns@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
